MCHOWL'S NAVY

Information

Hello! This website was created to celebrate the paranormal sitcom McHowl's Navy, which aired on CBS from 1968-1971. It was never the #1 hit, but it was always popular until it was cancelled following the murder of its producer, Esteban Lupetto, and it's still beloved by us!. During the first two seasons, it was on at 7:30 PM on Fridays, but during the third season it was on at 6 PM on Tuesdays.

Characters

Captain Harry McHowl
The captain of the USS Dogfish. By day, he's a normal submarine captain. By night, he's a normal submarine captain, usually. But during full moons, he's a werewolf who has to hide his secret from his crew!
USS Dogfish
Captain McHowl's loyal submarine. It has traveled all over the world and under the seas.
Commander Fullman
The Dogfish's first officer, who manages the crew and the captain.
Chief Petty Officer Gatito
A senior crewman who tries to steer the Dogfish out of trouble, but sometimes misses and steers into it instead.
The Crew
The rest of the crew of the Dogfish is a merry bunch, until the teeth come out.
Growlboater
A rival werewolf who's threatening the Dogfish and her crew!
