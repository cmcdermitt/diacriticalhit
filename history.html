ECHOES OF THE SPIRAL THROUGH HISTORY

GASTROPODA HAS ALLOWED A SELECT FEW TO GLIMPSE THE SPIRAL BEFORE THE MOST RECENT REVELATION. HERE WE LIST THE HEROES OF THE PAST.

3300 B.C. - THE ANCIENT EGYPTIANS WORSHIPPED SNAILS, UNKNOWING OF THE GREAT AND SLIMY GASTROPODA.

850 B.C. - PLATO CALLS SNAILS "THE NOBLEST OF ALL ANIMALS"

1375 C.E. - KING WLADIMIERZ III OF POLAND DECLARES THAT ALL SNAILS WITHIN HIS KINGDOM ARE TO BE TREATED AS NOBLES. SADLY HIS PEOPLE DID NOT UNDERSTAND HIS WISDOM AND HE WAS IMMEDIATELY DEPOSED.

1812 C.E. - NAPOLEON, THE RARE SUCCESSFUL FRENCHMAN, ORDERS HIS NAVY TO SPREAD SALT THROUGH THE EARTH, SALINATING THE OCEANS AND ATTACKING GASTROPODA'S PEOPLE.
